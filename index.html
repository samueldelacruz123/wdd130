Nueva Vizcaya

Nueva Vizcaya is a province in Northern Luzon and it is known for the Citrus Capital of the Philippines. Most peeople earn a living here by agriculture or planting rice, corn, fruits and vegetables. The province is surrounded by mountains that helps reduce the wind speeds of hurricanes and storms that goes through. People here are still very cultural and are still hosting grand festivals and other cultural activities. And the climate is really pleasant.
